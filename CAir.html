What is CAir?

CAir is a comprehensive Air Quality & Environmental Monitoring Platform that provides real-time insights into air quality and essential environmental conditions across diverse locations.
With dynamic and interactive dashboards, users can track pollutants, temperature, real feel, visibility, wind speed, UV index, humidity, pressure, and sunrise/sunset times, making it an all-in-one solution.
CAir empowers individuals to make informed decisions for healthier living, safer outdoor planning, and increased environmental awareness.
